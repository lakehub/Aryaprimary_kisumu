About Computer Lab

The school has a fully equipped computer lab with well over 30 computers. All pupils are free to use these computers which have 24 hours internet access.
We are the pioneers in e-learning in this region. Our computers are all installed with e-learning materials. We have a laptop, projector and sound system which are used for classroom broadcast of e-syllabus.
We have a full time qualified computer lecturer who helps pupils with computer studies ande-learningmaterials management.
